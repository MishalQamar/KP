Collaborating with Government, Building for Citizens

For over a decade, Code for Pakistan, in partnership with the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa IT Board (KPITB), has been transforming public services through the six-month Government Innovation Fellowship Program. By embedding technologists within government departments, the Fellowship enhances digital governance, builds citizen trust, and improves public service delivery.
